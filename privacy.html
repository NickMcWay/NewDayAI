Privacy Statement

NewDay is built to help you focus on meaningful work while respecting your privacy. We only collect information needed to provide and improve core app functionality.

Data we process

  • Account details you provide for sign-in and support.
  • Task and planning content you create inside the app.
  • Basic diagnostic information to keep the service reliable and secure.

How data is used

  • To deliver your planning and focus experience across devices.
  • To protect accounts and prevent abuse.
  • To improve app quality and performance.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be shared with trusted service providers only when needed to run NewDay (for example hosting, analytics, and customer support).

Your choices

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting the NewDay team. You can also uninstall the app at any time.
