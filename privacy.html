Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how NewDay (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the NewDay iOS app and related website.

1) Information we collect

  • Account Information: Email or sign-in details required to create and secure your account.
  • User Content: Tasks, plans, notes, area organization, and other productivity information you create in the app.
  • Diagnostics: Device/app performance data, crash reports, and logs used to maintain reliability and security.

2) How we use information

  • Provide core app functionality and sync your data across supported devices.
  • Authenticate users, prevent abuse, and protect account security.
  • Fix bugs, improve performance, and develop product features.
  • Respond to support requests and service communications.

3) Tracking and advertising

We do not sell personal information. We do not use your NewDay task content for third-party advertising.

4) Sharing of information

We may share limited information with service providers that help us operate NewDay (for example cloud hosting, analytics, and support tooling), subject to contractual confidentiality and security obligations.

5) Data retention

We retain data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

6) Your choices and rights

  • You may request access, correction, export, or deletion of your personal data.
  • You may delete the app at any time.
  • You can contact us regarding privacy requests at 3dlight.mail@gmail.com.

7) Children’s privacy

NewDay is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8) Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect personal information.

9) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material updates will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.

10) Support URL

Support and privacy requests can be sent to 3dlight.mail@gmail.com.
